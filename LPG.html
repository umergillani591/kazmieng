LPG Gas Sales & Distribution

Kazmi Engineering is a trusted name in the sale and distribution of high-quality LPG across Pakistan.

We proudly supply LPG to leading companies such as Universal Foods, SM Foods, and many others, ensuring consistent quality, safety, and timely delivery.

We also offer certified commercial and domestic LPG cylinders, tailored to meet the diverse energy needs of industries, businesses, and households. Our product portfolio includes:

  • • Domestic Cylinders: : Ranging from 5.0 kg to 15.0 kg capacity, perfect for household use.
  • • Commercial Cylinders: Robust and certified cylinders designed for industrial and commercial applications.
  • • Customized Solutions: Supply of LPG in bulk and tailored cylinder packages based on client requirements.

Our focus is on providing reliable energy solutions that empower businesses and enrich everyday life — safely, efficiently, and responsibly.
