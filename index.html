Overview

Kazmi Engineering was founded in 1995 in response to safety violations and the lack of technical expertise in the growing LPG sector. The first LPG quota was issued in 1972–73 from the Dhullian Oil Field, and as the industry expanded, the need for experienced professionals became critical. With strong field experience, Kazmi Engineering aimed to ensure safe, ethical, and efficient LPG plant installations.
The company quickly built a solid reputation for quality service, safety standards, and ethical practices. Today, it serves marketing companies across all provinces, contributing to the energy sector with commitment and integrity. The founder takes pride in the firm’s positive impact and remains dedicated to its continued growth and responsible service.

Our Services

Consultancy & Feasibility Studies

Kazmi offers expert guidance for new and existing LPG projects. They assess technical, financial, and safety aspects. Helps clients avoid risks and make informed decisions. Great for startups and expanding LPG businesses.

Expansion Projects

Kazmi upgrades and expands existing LPG facilities safely and efficiently. They design scalable solutions that fit current setups. New systems integrate smoothly without disrupting operations. Ideal for companies looking to increase capacity.

Requalification of Vessels

Kazmi Engineering performs certified inspections on storage tanks and bowsers, ensuring safety and compliance. Only qualified vessels are reapproved for use.

Revalidation of Plants

Kazmi Engineering offers safety checks and system upgrades for aging LPG plants. They inspect for wear, damage, and outdated parts, ensuring compliance and reducing risks. Revalidation boosts both safety and performance.

LPG Auto Refueling Stations

Kazmi Engineering designs and builds LPG auto refueling stations in Pakistan and Afghanistan, offering planning, installation, safety systems, and staff training. Each station is customized for local needs while meeting safety and environmental standards.

Commissioning of LPG Plants

Kazmi Engineering manages the complete setup and testing of LPG plants, ensuring safety and functionality. This includes trial runs, system checks, and staff training for smooth operations. Their engineers oversee the process until the plant is fully ready for safe use.
