Message from the CEO

The first LPG quota allocation by the Ministry of Petroleum was granted to Foundation Gas & Burshane Pak Ltd in 1972-73 from the Dhullian Oil Field. As the LPG business expanded, more licenses were issued, but safety violations increased, highlighting the need for technical expertise in plant installation. With years of field experience, I founded Kazmi Engineering in 1995 to serve the LPG sector with proper knowledge and commitment.

Kazmi Engineering earned a solid reputation by operating ethically, prioritizing safety, and choosing the right products. By the grace of Allah, the firm is now well-recognized nationwide, with offices across all provinces, serving major marketing companies that rely on our experience.

I take pride in the positive change we've brought to the energy sector. I'm grateful for our growth and remain committed to continuing this mission with hard work, ethics, loyalty, and care. I truly believe I’m doing what I was meant to do, and I’m thankful for the opportunity to make a meaningful impact.
