Who We Are

Kazmi Engineering is a leading LPG engineering firm in Pakistan, with offices in Rawalpindi, Karachi, and Lahore. Since 1994, we’ve specialized in LPG plant design, installation, inspection, maintenance, and consultancy.

We’ve served international clients in Bahrain and Kenya, offering expert solutions in collaboration with global partners. Our skilled team, modern facilities, and in-house tools ensure quality service and efficiency.

Committed to professionalism and safety, we deliver reliable engineering services with long-term client support.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a global leader in LPG engineering services, offering innovative solutions and consultancy to clients both domestically and internationally. We aspire to expand our reach, collaborate with international firms, and contribute to the development of LPG infrastructure in emerging markets, while upholding our core values of truth, loyalty, and excellence.

Our Mission

Kazmi Engineering is dedicated to delivering high-quality services within the specified timeframes, ensuring safety and health standards are met in every project. Our mission is to provide exceptional LPG engineering services while maintaining professionalism, dedication, and adherence to ethical practices. We aim to exceed customer expectations through meticulous planning, efficient execution, and continuous support even after the project’s completion.

Core Values

Kazmi Engineering upholds the highest standards of integrity, professionalism, and customer satisfaction, ensuring every service we provide meets our clients' needs with excellence, safety, and trust.
